RandomHorizon

Digital Artist, Musician and Founder of Saviour Project.

I am a digital artist and musician from Ontario Canada. Born and raised in Ridgetown, i found the love of art and music at an early age.
At the age of 16 i began my journey as a self-taught musician. Begining with 6 string guitar, i moved into keyboard and audio mixing from there.
As a physical or classic artist i began with sketching and moved into on-paper graffiti which evolved into using spray can art tagging abandoned buildings and walls in the community.
In the last 5 years I have found a new love in digital artforms. Using an Ipad and Procreate i started cartooning and digital oil painting. On the discovery of web3 and blockchain
I began expiramenting and working with NFT projects as an artist and eventually founded my own project, Saviour Project. Focusing on helping those suffering with mental health we set out
to construct metaverse hubs for those seeking a new avanue of medical attention as well as education, which is currently what i have been working on for the past 9 months to bring to fruition.
